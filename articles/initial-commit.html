Initial Thoughts

July 11 2025, 1 min read, ai tech

This is my personal website, where I will (probably very infrequently) publish articles and reflections on various tech topics.

Most articles today are AI-written and include too many empty words to increase the read time. The issue is actually not the length, but the content. It is just painful to read most of them, and I end up skipping to the end trying to find the punchline or conclusions.

One of my favourite tech articles of all time is grug brained developer. It is not short, but all the ideas are presented in a funny way, with as few words as possible.

I may not succeed, but this will be my modus operandi: short reflections on topics that interest me, without AI-generated fluff or nonsense.

Why git log

This statement encompasses what I would like from all my articles (if possible --online version). It provides clarity and shows important information in a clear and consise way.
